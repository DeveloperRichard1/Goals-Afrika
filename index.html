Who We Are

Goals Afrika is a network of Youth across Africa who are passionate about the SDGs and in building an African community. Goals Afrika is for and by Youth.

Our Vision

To collaboratively engage with citizens all over Africa in achieving Sustainable Development Goals on the continent
