Events and projects in Mozambique




INFO

A fundraise was done in Mozambique to aggregate refugee from Cabo Delgado whereas the donation come form 6: Maputo city, Maputo Province, Gaza, Sofala, Tete and Niassa) receiving clothes. To help contribute the reduction of waste, Mozambique have been working with volunteers. They were able to inquiry people near schools, to better understand the major problems that Mozambique is facing such as the listed below. the Mozambican volunteers promoted an online workshop related to compost, later published on Facebook page. The aim was to teach young Mozambicans how to make compost (fertilizer) by using organic waste from our kitchen allowing to have organic crops in our small farms and to show that there are ways to optimize the use of waste we think is garbage. Compost = organic fertilizer = cheap and better crops. On the other hand, a fundraise was done for Cabo Delgado including 6 provinces: Maputo city, Maputo Province, Gaza, Sofala, Tete and Niassa) receiving clothes. Among Mozambique, inquire was made for more than 300 people on which we had 114 answers and raised up to 119 recently. The graphics below show the number of volunteers per province as well the average age of volunteers. Moreover, a fundraise campaign for clothes was done in 6 provinces (Maputo City, Maputo Province, Gaza, Sofala, Tete and Niassa). A refugee center was in Nampula province, and the clothes should be distributed for them. In total we acquired almost 90kg of clothes for adults and children.
